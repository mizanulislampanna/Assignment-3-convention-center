An Event Planner, or Event Manager, is responsible for organizing corporate or private events for companies or individuals. Their duties include meeting with clients to discuss their needs, maintaining partnerships with vendors and caterers and overseeing the set-up, execution and cleanup of events.

Planning an event can take from weeks to months, it really depends on the size and the details that the event involves. Having enough time allows you to comfortably cover different areas and prepare yourself for potential scenarios that might happen so you can consider various options prior the date. You always need more time than you think!With our experience, planning private and corporate events requires a shorter period of time because guests usually have already confirmed their attendance and no details are needed to be released to the public. The only things that need planning are the event elements such as catering, entertainment, technical and venue.
